Bipolar disorder

What is bipolar disorder?

Highly heritable, lifelong vulnerability that destabilises mood regulation. Compromised prefrontal-limbic regulation, hypersensitivity to dopaminergic effects, intracellular signalling, circadian biology. Kindling then degeneration. Highly complex. Many overlaps with other psychiatric conditions such as schizophrenia and major depression
